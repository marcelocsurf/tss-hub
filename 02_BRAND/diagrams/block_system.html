The 8 Learning Blocks

Block 0
Orientation & Surf Literacy
Foundation before everything. Understanding water dynamics, safety, and wave structure.
Block 1
Preparation & Positioning
Identifying the lineup, finding the sweet spot, reading the break pattern.
Block 2
Wave Entry
Paddling angle, chase mechanics, picking the line, commitment to the wave.
Block 3
Pop-Up & Board Connection
The explosive stand, feet positioning, initial rail engagement, balance on the board.
Once Standing, The Infinite Circle Begins
Block 4
Power Posture
Foundation of all cyclic movement. Stance, weight distribution, core engagement.
Block 5
Rotation / Rail Change / Bottom Turn
Redirecting energy, engaging the turn, loading the rail, initiating maneuvers.
Block 6
Projection
Throwing energy up the face, generating speed, launching into expression.
Block 7
Maneuver / Expression
Snap, reentry, carve, cut, or aerial. The creative application of all blocks in one moment.

Blocks do not replace sequences. Blocks are functional components.

Blocks do not replace belts. Belts are progressional pathways.

Belt-specific sequences are BUILT using blocks. Each sequence combines blocks strategically to develop specific skills.
