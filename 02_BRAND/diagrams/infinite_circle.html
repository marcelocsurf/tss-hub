The Infinite Cycle

Blocks 1-3 are sequential: they happen once per wave, in order. Entry is linear and unrepeatable — the surfer positions, enters, and stands.

Blocks 4-7 are cyclical: once standing, the surfer repeats the cycle as many times as the wave allows. Blocks 4-7 can be looped, stacked, and combined to create endless maneuver variations.

This is The Infinite Circle.
