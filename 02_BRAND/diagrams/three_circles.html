Three Circles of Power

Surfer Version

Body: P·R·C·H

  • Posture — foundational stance and alignment
  • Rotation — torso and hip engagement
  • Compression — knee and ankle flex
  • Hold — grip and pressure control

Board: Feet Position

  • FP1: Full Tail — maximum control, tight turns
  • FP2: Neutral — balance point, flow state
  • FP3: Forward — acceleration, drive, projection

Wave: Dynamics

  • Energy — power and momentum in the section
  • Direction — line selection and angle
  • Section — identifying the next expressive zone
  • Pocket — the sweet spot for maximum performance

Coach Version

Surfer

  • Level — current belt rank and progression
  • Requirements — what skills must be demonstrated
  • Belt Band — the zone for targeted development

Environment

  • Wave Size — shoulder to head+ to double overhead
  • Wind & Swell — direction, period, consistency
  • Break Type — beach break, reef, point break

Task / Game

  • Objective — the specific skill or maneuver to master
  • Constraints — rules, zones, or limitations
  • Feedback — real-time information and cues

Foundational Language & Diagnostic Lens

The Three Circles are the foundational language and diagnostic lens of the method.

The Block System is the functional architecture of surfing.

The Infinite Circle is the operational loop that repeats during every ride.

Together, they form a complete, interconnected system for teaching, coaching, and mastering surfing.
