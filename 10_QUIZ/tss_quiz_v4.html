Why your real level matters

Training above your level doesn't make you progress faster — it creates gaps in your foundation that show up later as plateaus, bad habits, and frustration.

The surfers who improve the fastest are the ones who own where they are and train the specific skills that unlock the next level. There's no shortcut — but there is an optimal sequence.
