SECTION 1: IP OWNERSHIP DECLARATION

This Declaration establishes exclusive intellectual property ownership by Enkrateia SA de CV for all components and derivatives of The Surf Sequence methodology and system.

1.1 Exclusive Ownership Statement

Enkrateia SA de CV, owned 100% by Marcelo Castellanos, declares and certifies that it owns exclusively, without limitation, restriction, or encumbrance, all right, title, and interest in and to The Surf Sequence, including but not limited to all intellectual property, whether patentable, copyrightable, or trade secret in nature.

1.2 Protected Assets

The intellectual property assets owned by Enkrateia SA de CV include, without limitation:

(a) The Surf Sequence Methodology - the complete educational system, pedagogical framework, and instructional approach;

(b) Canon v5.0 - the foundational principles and technical specifications governing the methodology;

(c) Belt Progression System - all levels (white, yellow, orange, green, blue, purple, brown, black) and advancement criteria;

(d) The 26 Sequences - all programmed progressions, drills, exercises, and related instructional content;

(e) Expression Names and Terminology - all proprietary names, terms, and descriptive language unique to TSS;

(f) TSS Brain Platform - the digital platform, software, database architecture, user interface, and all related technology;

(g) Brand Identity - the name "The Surf Sequence®", all variations, taglines, and associated branding elements;

(h) Logo and Visual Identity - the Fibonacci spiral logo, all design variations, and visual representations;

(i) Tagline and Messaging - "Evolve through play" and all associated marketing copy and brand messaging;

(j) Training Materials - all coach manuals, educational documents, instructional guides, and training resources;

(k) Drill Library - the complete collection of documented drills, exercises, and training progressions;

(l) Certification System - all criteria, standards, testing procedures, and certification materials;

(m) Data and Analytics - all aggregated student data, progression metrics, and system analytics;

(n) Derivative Works and Improvements - all improvements, modifications, variations, and derivatives created during or after the development of TSS.

1.3 Status of Operators and Licensees

Puro Surf and any other entity operating under The Surf Sequence brand are LICENSEES and OPERATORS only. These entities:

(a) Do NOT own any intellectual property rights in The Surf Sequence;

(b) Do NOT have the right to sublicense, assign, or transfer any TSS intellectual property;

(c) May only use TSS materials as expressly permitted by written license agreement;

(d) Must comply with all quality standards, branding guidelines, and operational requirements;

(e) Must return or destroy all TSS materials upon termination of the license.

1.4 Clarification: No Rights Created by Affiliation

Notwithstanding any collaboration, operational partnership, revenue sharing, or other business relationship, no employment, partnership, equity interest, affiliation, or other relationship shall create, imply, or grant any ownership right, intellectual property right, or license right in The Surf Sequence except as expressly stated in a written agreement executed by Enkrateia SA de CV.

1.5 Registered and Unregistered IP

Enkrateia SA de CV asserts ownership of all The Surf Sequence intellectual property, whether or not formally registered with trademark offices, copyright offices, or other governmental bodies. The absence of registration does not diminish or limit these ownership rights.

For legal protection, Enkrateia SA de CV intends to seek registration of all core intellectual property assets including trademarks, copyrights, and potentially patents in all jurisdictions where TSS operates or may operate.

SECTION 2: COACH CERTIFICATION AGREEMENT (L1-L5)

This Agreement grants Coach the non-transferable, non-exclusive right to teach The Surf Sequence methodology under the terms and conditions herein.

2.1 License Grant

Enkrateia SA de CV grants Coach a limited, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to teach The Surf Sequence at Coach's affiliated academy (as specified in Academy Affiliation Agreement). This license is personal to the named Coach and may not be transferred, assigned, or delegated.

2.2 Certification Levels (L1-L5)

Coach shall maintain one of the following certification levels:

(a) Level 1: Foundation Coach - Can teach beginner sequences under L3+ supervision.

(b) Level 2: Intermediate Coach - Can teach beginner and intermediate sequences independently.

(c) Level 3: Advanced Coach - Can teach all student levels, responsible for academy quality.

(d) Level 4: Master Coach - Can train and certify new coaches, develop academy curriculum.

(e) Level 5: Master Instructor - Can develop new TSS content, lead methodology evolution, represent TSS globally.

2.3 Annual Renewal Requirement

This certification expires on the anniversary date each year. Coach must renew certification annually by:

(a) Submitting renewal application to Enkrateia SA de CV;

(b) Demonstrating continued compliance with quality standards;

(c) Paying annual renewal fee (amount set by Enkrateia SA de CV);

(d) Completing any required continuing education or updates.

2.4 Quality Standards

Coach agrees to maintain the following quality standards:

(a) Teaching all sequences according to TSS Canon v5.0 without modification;

(b) Maintaining safety standards as specified in TSS Canon v2.0;

(c) Properly assessing student progression and only advancing students who meet criteria;

(d) Providing professional instruction and maintaining student engagement;

(e) Properly representing TSS branding and maintaining brand standards;

(f) Responding to audits, quality assessments, and student feedback.

2.5 Prohibition on Modification and Derivative Works

Coach is strictly prohibited from:

(a) Modifying, adapting, translating, or altering any TSS sequence, drill, or methodology;

(b) Creating derivative works, variations, or alternative approaches based on TSS;

(c) Combining TSS with other methodologies without express written approval;

(d) Using TSS materials to develop competing or alternative education systems.

2.6 Prohibition on Coach Training (Below L4)

Coaches at Levels 1-3 are explicitly prohibited from:

(a) Training, instructing, or certifying other individuals in The Surf Sequence;

(b) Conducting coach certification programs;

(c) Authorizing or supervising coach training by others.

Only Level 4 and Level 5 coaches, with express written authorization from Enkrateia SA de CV, may train and certify new coaches.

2.7 Confidentiality of Internal Materials

Coach acknowledges that all TSS materials are proprietary and confidential, including but not limited to:

(a) Coach manuals and training guides;

(b) The complete drill library and exercise specifications;

(c) Detailed sequence progressions and teaching notes;

(d) Assessment criteria and belt progression standards;

(e) Any digital platform access credentials or backend systems.

Coach must protect these materials and may not disclose them to any third party without express written consent of Enkrateia SA de CV.

2.8 Termination Conditions

Enkrateia SA de CV may terminate this certification for:

(a) Quality violations or failure to maintain standards;

(b) Non-payment of renewal fees or other required fees;

(c) Misuse of TSS brand or intellectual property;

(d) Breach of confidentiality or disclosure of proprietary information;

(e) Violation of non-compete or other agreement terms;

(f) Failure to renew certification or pay annual fees;

(g) Any material breach of this Agreement.

2.9 Non-Compete During Active Certification

During the period of active certification, Coach agrees not to:

(a) Develop, teach, or promote any competing surf education methodology;

(b) Create an alternative belt or progression system;

(c) Teach or promote methods explicitly designed to compete with TSS.

2.10 Post-Termination Obligations

Upon termination of certification for any reason, Coach must within 30 days:

(a) Cease using the TSS name, trademarks, and brand in teaching or communications;

(b) Stop teaching The Surf Sequence methodology;

(c) Return or destroy all TSS proprietary materials;

(d) Remove all TSS certifications from credentials and marketing;

(e) Cooperate in transition of any students to certified TSS coaches.

2.11 IP Assignment of Improvements

Any improvements, refinements, variations, or derivative content created by Coach while under certification shall automatically become the exclusive property of Enkrateia SA de CV. Coach hereby assigns all right, title, and interest in such improvements to Enkrateia SA de CV.

2.12 Indemnification

Coach agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Enkrateia SA de CV, its officers, employees, and agents from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from Coach's instruction, actions, or breaches of this Agreement.

3.4 Quality Audits and Compliance

Enkrateia SA de CV reserves the right to conduct quality audits, in-person visits, and evaluations at any time to ensure:

(a) Sequences are taught according to Canon v5.0;

(b) Safety standards are maintained;

(b) Cease all use of TSS methodology and teaching materials;

SECTION 5: STUDENT ENROLLMENT AGREEMENT

This Agreement sets forth the terms under which students may enroll in The Surf Sequence programs.

5.1 Assumption of Risk

Student acknowledges and assumes all risks associated with surfing, including but not limited to:

(a) Physical injury from falls, collisions with board or environment, or impact with other surfers;

(b) Water-related hazards including drowning, cold water exposure, currents, and weather conditions;

(c) Environmental hazards including rocks, reefs, marine life, and underwater obstacles;

(d) Injuries resulting from failure to follow instructions or safety protocols.

Student accepts and assumes these risks as inherent to surfing and TSS training.

5.2 Liability Waiver and Release

In consideration for participation in TSS programs, Student voluntarily waives, releases, and discharges The Surf Sequence, Enkrateia SA de CV, the Operating Academy, coaches, and related personnel from any and all claims, demands, or causes of action arising from Student's participation, including claims for:

(a) Personal injury or property damage;

(b) Negligence or breach of duty;

(c) Any economic or non-economic damages.

This waiver shall apply to the extent permitted by law.

5.3 Medical Clearance

Student represents that they are physically fit to participate in TSS programs and have disclosed any medical conditions, injuries, or medications that may affect participation. If Student is a minor, parent/guardian warrants Student's fitness and assumes liability on Student's behalf.

5.4 Media Release

Student consents to the recording, photography, and video recording of Student during TSS classes and events. Student authorizes The Surf Sequence and Operating Academy to use such images and recordings for:

(a) TSS marketing and promotional purposes;

(b) TSS social media and website content;

(c) Instructional and quality assessment purposes.

Student may opt out of promotional use by providing written notice.

5.5 Privacy Policy and Data Handling

Student's personal information (name, contact info, medical information) is used solely for TSS program administration. Data may be shared with:

(a) The Operating Academy for program delivery;

(b) Enkrateia SA de CV for aggregated analytics and quality improvement;

(c) Emergency services if necessary for Student safety.

Student data shall not be sold to third parties.

5.6 Belt Progression and Merit-Based Advancement

Student acknowledges that:

(a) Belt progression in TSS is MERIT-BASED and requires demonstrated mastery of sequences and criteria;

(b) No advancement is guaranteed regardless of time spent or fees paid;

(c) Coaches are the sole judges of whether Student meets progression criteria;

(d) Student may be required to repeat levels if competency is not demonstrated.

5.7 Fees and Refund Policy

Student agrees to pay:

(a) Enrollment Fee: $[Amount] (non-refundable);

(b) Monthly/Package Tuition: $[Amount] due [Payment Schedule];

(c) Belt Test Fees: $[Amount] per belt progression test.

Refund Policy: Refunds are not provided for:

• Lessons not attended;

• Classes cancelled due to weather or environmental conditions;

• Student withdrawal;

• Lessons missed due to Student illness or injury.

5.8 Code of Conduct

Student agrees to:

(a) Treat coaches, staff, and fellow students with respect;

(b) Follow all safety instructions and protocols;

(c) Maintain appropriate behavior both in water and on land;

(d) Not use illegal substances before or during training;

(e) Report any safety concerns immediately to coaches.

Violation of code of conduct may result in suspension or permanent dismissal without refund.

5.9 Acknowledgment and Signature

By enrolling in TSS programs, Student (or parent/guardian if minor) acknowledges having read this entire agreement, understanding all terms, and agreeing to be bound by all provisions.

SECTION 6: FLAGSHIP OPERATOR AGREEMENT (PURO SURF)

Puro Surf is designated as the "TSS Flagship Academy," meaning:

(a) Puro Surf is the primary operational hub for The Surf Sequence;

(b) Puro Surf demonstrates exemplary TSS standards and may serve as a training location for new coaches;

(c) Puro Surf may participate in TSS methodology development and innovation;

(d) Puro Surf receives marketing support and recognition as the official flagship academy.

6.2 Critical Clarification: Puro Surf OPERATES TSS, Does Not OWN TSS

Puro Surf, its owners, and its employees must clearly understand and acknowledge:

(a) Puro Surf is a LICENSEE and OPERATOR of The Surf Sequence;

(b) Enkrateia SA de CV owns 100% of all TSS intellectual property;

(c) No operational contributions, management decisions, or financial investments by Puro Surf create ownership rights;

(d) No partnership, joint venture, or affiliation exists between Puro Surf and Enkrateia SA de CV regarding IP ownership;

(e) The license may be terminated at any time with appropriate notice;

(f) Upon termination, Puro Surf must immediately cease all use of TSS IP.

6.3 License Grant

Enkrateia SA de CV grants Puro Surf a limited license to:

(a) Use the TSS name, brand, logo, and methodology;

(b) Operate TSS programs at designated location(s);

(c) Market as "TSS Flagship Academy";

(d) Train certified coaches.

6.4 Revenue Sharing or Licensing Fee Structure

Puro Surf shall pay to Enkrateia SA de CV:

(a) [Option 1 - Revenue Share]: [X]% of gross revenue from TSS programs;

(b) [Option 2 - Fixed License Fee]: $[Amount] annually;

(c) [Option 3 - Hybrid]: Combination of fixed fee plus revenue share above threshold;

(d) Per-coach certification renewal fees: $[Amount] per coach annually.

All payments due [Payment Schedule]. Failure to pay constitutes material breach.

6.5 IP Ownership - Crystal Clear Statement

Notwithstanding any operational success, financial contribution, or business development by Puro Surf:

(a) Enkrateia SA de CV retains 100% ownership of all TSS intellectual property;

(b) Puro Surf acquires NO ownership rights through operation;

(c) IP ownership is NOT shared, transferred, or diminished by this Agreement;

(d) This arrangement is purely a license, not a partnership or joint venture.

6.6 Improvements and Methodology Enhancement

Any improvements, new drills, teaching approaches, or enhancements to TSS developed or tested by Puro Surf shall:

(a) Be immediately disclosed to Enkrateia SA de CV;

(b) Become exclusive property of Enkrateia SA de CV;

(c) Be incorporated into global TSS at Enkrateia SA de CV's sole discretion.

6.7 Marketing Rights and Co-Branding Rules

Puro Surf may:

(a) Use "TSS Flagship Academy" designation in marketing;

(b) Use TSS logo in co-branded materials with Enkrateia SA de CV approval;

(c) Market as an authorized TSS facility.

Puro Surf may NOT:

(a) Create derivative branding or modified logos;

(b) Claim ownership of TSS in any marketing;

(c) Make statements implying TSS is jointly owned or co-created;

(d) License TSS to other entities without written authorization.

6.8 Quality Standards and Audits

As Flagship Academy, Puro Surf must maintain the highest TSS quality standards and be subject to:

(a) Quarterly quality audits by Enkrateia SA de CV;

(b) Student satisfaction assessments;

(c) Coach performance reviews;

(d) Ongoing compliance monitoring.

6.9 Financial Reporting and Audit Rights

Puro Surf shall provide:

(a) Monthly revenue reports;

(b) Quarterly financial statements;

(c) Annual audited financials;

(d) Full access to books and records for audit by Enkrateia SA de CV.

6.10 Termination Provisions

(a) Term: Initial term of [3-5] years, renewable at mutual agreement;

(b) Termination for Cause: Immediate termination for material breach (non-payment, quality violations, IP misuse);

(c) Termination for Convenience: Enkrateia SA de CV may terminate with [6-12] months notice;

(d) Termination Fee: Puro Surf may owe transition or wind-down fees as agreed.

6.11 Transition Plan Upon Termination

Upon termination, Puro Surf shall:

(a) Cease use of TSS name, logo, and brand immediately;

(b) Transfer all student data and records to Enkrateia SA de CV or successor operator;

(c) Facilitate student transition to other TSS academies;

(d) Return all proprietary materials, training guides, and platform access;

(e) Cooperate fully with successor operator.

6.12 Indemnification

Puro Surf indemnifies Enkrateia SA de CV from all claims arising from Puro Surf's operation, student injuries, or breaches, with no exception for IP misuse or ownership disputes.
