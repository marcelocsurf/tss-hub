The problem for surf academies today

No Differentiation

All schools offer the same: "surf lessons with certified instructors." No way to stand out in the market.

Coach Dependency

Quality depends 100% on who's teaching that day. If your best coach leaves, quality drops immediately.

No Continuity

Student comes in, takes a lesson, leaves. No reason to return. No visible progression. No retention.

The Solution

What TSS brings to your academy

Standardized Methodology

Complete curriculum per level. Every coach teaches the same with the same quality. Experience doesn't depend on one person.

Certified Coaches

Your team gets certified in TSS (L1-L5). Certification with real requirements that guarantee teaching quality.

Digital Platform (TSS Brain)

Student progression tracking, session management, automatic reports, real-time data.

Belt System = Retention

Students have a reason to come back: their next belt. Visible progression builds commitment and increases average ticket.

Market Differentiation

You're the only academy in your area with global standard, Olympic validation, and professional certification system.

Global Network

Connection with other affiliated academies. Students can continue their progression anywhere in the network.
