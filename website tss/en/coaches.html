Certification

5 TSS Certification Levels

Each level enables you to teach specific belts and access more advanced system tools.

Level L1 — Assistant Coach

Requirement: Current lifeguard certification

Assist in supervised sessions. Master basic safety protocols. Can work with White Belt under direct supervision.

Level L2 — Basic Coach

Requirement: L1 + ISA Level 1

Lead White Belt and Yellow Belt sessions independently. Master the complete TSS Block. Evaluate initial level progression.

Level L3 — Intermediate Coach

Requirement: L2 + ISA Level 2

Teach up to Blue Belt. Manage mixed-level groups. Master alignment formations (Pair, Triangle, Magnet).

Level L4 — Advanced Coach

Requirement: L3 + documented experience

Teach up to Purple Belt. Train L1-L2 coaches. Design seasonal training programs. Evaluate for certification.

Level L5 — Expert Coach / Trainer

Requirement: L4 + system contribution

Master all levels. Form and certify coaches. Contribute to methodology development. Can direct affiliated academies.
