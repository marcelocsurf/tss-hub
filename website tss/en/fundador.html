Founder

Marcelo Castellanos

ISA Olympic coach. Learning systems architect. Coach developer. Creator of The Surf Sequence®.

Philosophy

Marcelo believes that surfing is more than a sport — it's a tool for integral development. But without structure, the potential is wasted. TSS is born from the conviction that surfing deserves the same educational rigor as any other serious discipline. That fun and science are not opposites, but allies. That true mastery is not just dominating the board, but being able to transmit that knowledge to others.
