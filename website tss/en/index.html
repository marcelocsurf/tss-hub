Progression

6 Belts. One clear path. No shortcuts.

Each belt has its sequences, ocean conditions, and evaluation criteria. You don't level up by time or payment. You level up by demonstrated competence.

Level 1
White Belt

Foundational. Basic control, stance, pop-up, standing positions. Safety in shallow water.

1-2 foot waves
Level 2
Yellow Belt

Entry & Paddling. 4 paddle speeds, angled takeoff, lineup identification.

2-3 foot waves
Level 3
Blue Belt

Rail Changes & Maneuvers. 11 named expressions: Cross, Lip, Barrel, Bottom Turn.

4-6 foot waves
Level 4
Purple Belt

Advanced. 15 expressions: Floater, Snap, Tango, Layback, Tail Slide. Overhead conditions.

4-8 foot waves
Level 5
Brown Belt

Aerials. Complete aerial system: Aerial 360, Alley-oop, Power Combos. Variable conditions.

Variable conditions
Level 6
Black Belt

Mastery & Transmission. Integral mastery. The Infinite Circle: mastery = knowledge transmission.

All conditions
