Frequently Asked Questions

What you need to know

Do I need prior experience to start?

No. TSS starts at White Belt, which is absolute zero level. If you've never touched a board, there's a place for you.

How long does it take to level up?

Depends on your dedication and conditions. No fixed timeline. You level up when you demonstrate real competence in all your belt criteria.

Where can I train with TSS?

Currently at Puro Surf (El Zonte, El Salvador) as the flagship operator. The network of affiliated academies is expanding.

Is TSS only for competitors?

No. TSS is for any surfer who wants to progress with structure. From committed recreational to Olympic aspirant.

What's the difference between TSS and regular surf lessons?

A single lesson is an isolated event. TSS is a continuous progression system with evaluation, catalogued sequences, and a clear path from start to finish.
